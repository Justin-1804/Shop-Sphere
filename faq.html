↩️ Returns & Refunds
We offer a 7-day return window from the date of delivery. To qualify for a return, items must be:
  • Unused and in original condition
  • In original packaging with all accessories included
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (order ID or WhatsApp confirmation)
Perishable food items are not eligible for return unless the product arrived damaged or expired.
Simply message us on WhatsApp with your order ID and reason for return. We'll arrange a pickup (free for defective/wrong items, ₦500 for change-of-mind returns within Abeokuta). For customers outside Abeokuta, you'll ship the item back to us and we'll reimburse the shipping cost for valid defective/wrong-item returns.
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within 2–3 business days. For bank transfers, funds typically arrive within 24 hours of processing. We'll send you a WhatsApp notification when your refund is on the way.
We're so sorry if this happened! Please photograph the item and send the photos to our WhatsApp within 48 hours of delivery. 🏪 Selling on ShopSphere
Visit our Seller Dashboard and use the "Add New Product" form to list your products. There is no registration fee. Once you add a product, our team reviews it within 24 hours and either approves it or contacts you to discuss any issues. Approved products immediately appear in our shop.
Listing products is free. We operate on a commission model — we take a small percentage of each successful sale. Commission rates vary by category:
  • Electronics: 8%
  • Fashion & Accessories: 12%
  • Home & Living: 10%
  • Beauty: 12%
  • Food & Groceries: 6%
  • Books & Stationery: 8%
  • Sports: 10%
We accept new and quality pre-owned physical products across all our listed categories. We do not accept counterfeit goods, weapons, illegal substances, live animals, or any product that violates Nigerian law. All electronics must come with a minimum 3-month warranty. Food items must have valid NAFDAC numbers where applicable.
Seller payouts are processed every Friday for all orders confirmed and delivered in the previous week. Payments are made via bank transfer. You'll receive a WhatsApp notification with a breakdown of your weekly earnings. Minimum payout threshold is ₦5,000.
