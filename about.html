Our Story

From Abeokuta to the whole of Nigeria

ShopSphere started in 2021 as a small WhatsApp group run by our founder, Adeyemi Salami, selling imported electronics to friends and colleagues in Abeokuta. Within six months, demand had grown beyond what a group chat could handle — and a platform was born.

We built ShopSphere to solve a real problem: unreliable delivery, counterfeit products, and the frustrating experience of buying online in Nigeria. By combining curated product listings with a WhatsApp-first order system, we gave customers the familiarity of chatting with a trusted vendor — at marketplace scale.

Today, ShopSphere serves customers across all 36 states, with a catalogue spanning electronics, fashion, home essentials, beauty, sports equipment, food, and books. Every product on our platform is verified by our team before it goes live.

We remain headquartered in Abeokuta because we believe thriving local businesses are the backbone of Nigeria's future. Every order you place supports local logistics riders, warehouse staff, and entrepreneurs in Ogun State.

Core Values

What We Stand For

Our values aren't slogans on a wall. They shape every decision we make — from which products we approve to how we handle complaints.

✅
Authenticity First

We manually verify every seller and every product listing. Counterfeit goods are immediately removed, and sellers who misrepresent products are permanently banned.

🚀
Speed & Reliability

We obsess over delivery. Same-day in Abeokuta, 1–3 days to Lagos, 3–5 days nationwide. We track every package and proactively notify customers of any delay.

💬
Human Connection

We believe in the power of the human touch. Our WhatsApp-first order system means you're always talking to a real person, not a bot, when you need help.

🌱
Community Growth

We actively seek out and support micro-entrepreneurs and small businesses across Nigeria, helping them reach customers beyond their local area.

🔒
Data Privacy

Your personal information is never sold or shared with third parties for advertising. We collect only what is necessary to process your order and improve your experience.

↩️
No-Fuss Returns

Changed your mind or received a damaged item? Return it within 7 days — we cover the return logistics and issue a full refund or replacement, no questions asked.
